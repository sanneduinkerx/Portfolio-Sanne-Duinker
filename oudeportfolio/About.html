Over mij

Hi!

My name is Sanne, 19 years old. I’m currently studying Communication and Multimedia Design at the HvA (Hogeschool van Amsterdam), Currently i'm in my third year. What I love most about designing is the creativity, the visuals. Thinking about the identity of a product and make it one whole feeling and everything just feels right. Not only do I enjoy the visuals but I’m also a huge geek for coding. How everything is possible with HTML/CSS and Javascript. Don’t get me wrong coding is frustrating but that’s what I like about it. When you finally fixed that one problem or idea you got, that’s the best feeling. In my free time I do lots of creative stuff, I love reading books and playing around with photography.
